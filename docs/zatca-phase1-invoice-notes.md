# ZATCA QR scope

The CRM invoice QR currently implements ZATCA Phase 1 TLV fields for simplified tax invoices:

- Seller name.
- VAT registration number.
- Invoice timestamp in ISO format.
- Invoice total including VAT.
- VAT total.

This is not Phase 2 e-invoicing clearance or reporting. Phase 2 requires official integration with FATOORA, cryptographic signing, invoice hashing, CSID/certificate handling, and clearance/reporting responses from ZATCA.

Until Phase 2 is implemented and onboarded with ZATCA, invoices generated by the CRM should be treated as Phase 1 QR invoices only.
